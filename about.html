About Emly

Emly Nwankwo, professionally known as Emly, is a Nigerian R&B and Afrobeats singer-songwriter, born on June 30, 2004, in Choba, Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. A graduate of Ashesi University, Ghana, with a degree in Electrical Engineering.

EMLY's musical journey began at age 11, influenced by legendary artists Nina Simone, Amy Winehouse, Adele, and Jorja Smith. With her unique sound, EMLY embodies a fresh and compelling promise in the Afrobeats world.

She channels her emotions and experiences into creating smooth, soulful, and bold music, exploring genres like jazz, reggae, Latin music, Afrobeats, and R&B.

EMLY exudes quiet confidence and star power, with a reserved yet captivating old-soul personality. Her music is honest and true, inviting fans intoher emotional world, aiming to comfort and connect with her listeners.

What truly sets EMLY apart is her unique blend of soulful vocals, genre-bending style, authentic storytelling, cultural fusion, passionate performance, and unapologetically authentic personality

"I aim to create music that comfort and empowers, while embracing the beauty and struggles of love in all its form".

-Emly
